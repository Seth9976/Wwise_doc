SoundBanks Settings

The SoundBanks Settings dialog is where you define custom settings for your own personal SoundBanks. When you define custom user settings, you must override the corresponding Project Settings.

The SoundBanks Settings dialog is divided into two different tabs:

  • SoundBanks Settings - SoundBanks Tab

  • SoundBanks Settings - External Sources Tab
