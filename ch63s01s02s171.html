Segment look-ahead plus pre-entry duration is longer than previous segment in sequence.

This error occurs when the look-ahead setting cannot be honored by the Interactive Music system. This can result in music desynchronization.

Probable causes:

  • The duration of one streamed clip, including pre-entry, is smaller than the look-ahead time specified.

Recommended resolution steps:

  • Change the duration of the clip.

  • Change the look-ahead time. See Defining the playback behavior of Music Tracks.

  • Put the clip in a SoundBank (not streamed). See Managing SoundBanks.
