Stinger could not be scheduled in this segment or was dropped.

This error occurs when a Stinger is called by a Trigger and the next opportunity to play the Stinger occurs too late in the current (or next) segment, in which case the Stinger is dropped.

Recommended resolution step:

  • Set Allow playing Stinger in next segment in the Stinger properties.
