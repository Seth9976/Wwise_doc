Game Object 3D Viewer Keyboard Shortcuts

The following shortcuts are only available when the Game Object 3D Viewer is active.

To

Use this shortcut

Follows the located object with the camera as it moves during capture.

Ctrl+F

Reset the camera to its default position

Ctrl+R

Frame All game objects and listeners in the view.

Ctrl+A

Navigate forward (in Perspective camera)

Right-click+W/Up

Navigate backward (in Perspective camera)

Right-click+S/Down

Navigate left (in Perspective camera)

Right-click+A/Left

Navigate Right (in Perspective camera)

Right-click+D/Right

Navigate Up (in Perspective camera)

Right-click+E/Page Up

Navigate Down (in Perspective camera)

Right-click+Q/Page Down

Accelerate movement within the viewer

Shift

Switch to Perspective camera 1

Ctrl+1

Switch to Perspective camera 2

Ctrl+2

Switch to Top camera view

T

Switch to Bottom camera view

B

Switch to Left camera view

L

Switch to Right camera view

R

Switch to Front camera view

F

Switch to Back camera view

K
