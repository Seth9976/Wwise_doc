Schematic View

The Schematic view displays the hierarchy of the bus, motion, music, and sound objects in your project as a graphical representation. By default, the Schematic view displays the hierarchy and the different routing connections for your entire project, but you can use the Search field to filter the view and display the hierarchy for one object.

Note

In contrast to the Voices Graph, which does not display non-mixing busses, the Schematic view displays all busses, regardless of their processing status. Refer to Understanding the bus icons and processing status for more information on the various processing statuses.


Tip

By default in Wwise, you can switch to the Schematic layout by pressing F9.
