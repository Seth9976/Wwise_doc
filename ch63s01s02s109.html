Maximum Impulse Response length of hardware Convolution Reverb reached.

Pertains to AK Convolution Reverb. Hardware acceleration is enabled and the resulting Impulse Response length is greater than supported.

Recommended resolution steps:

  • Either disable hardware acceleration or adjust the value of the Reverb, Pre Delay parameter.
