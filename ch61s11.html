Presets

Presets are special files with saved property settings for an object, Effect, Positioning, Attenuation, or Metadata. Containers hierarchy: sound and motion objects, Containers hierarchy: music objects, Busses hierarchy, and ShareSet objects all have two clickable icons in the upper right corner of their respective Property Editors. The Effect Editor and Metadata Editor also have these icons.

Load Preset
Save Preset

These icons open the Load Preset dialog and the Save Preset dialog.
