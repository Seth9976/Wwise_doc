Common tabs and categories: music objects

Properties in Wwise are grouped into logical tabs in the Primary Editor and logical categories in the Property Editor. Both editors are found in Object Tabs. Across all music objects, the following tabs and categories have common properties:

  • States tab: music objects to define state values for particular music objects.

  • Transitions tab: music objects to define the transitions between music objects within either Music Playlist or Music Switch Containers.

  • Stingers tab: music objects to create and define Stingers, which are brief musical phrases that are superimposed and mixed over the currently playing music.

  • MIDI category: music objects to define the MIDI playback of music objects.

  • Advanced category: music objects to define the advanced playback priority of music objects.

Note

The Transitions tab is only available for Music Switch and Music Playlist Containers. The Stingers tab is available for all music objects except for music tracks.

Also see Common tabs and categories: audio structures for other tabs and properties common across multiple music objects.
