Reverting to Dummy Audio device (no output).

This message always appears after another error. It indicates that the audio output will not be routed to any physical device and will be ignored. There will be no sound at all. The rest of the operation of the sound engine will continue normally; processing Events, sounds, producing callbacks upon sound completion, etc. Check the preceding errors in the Capture Log and investigate those to resolve this issue.
