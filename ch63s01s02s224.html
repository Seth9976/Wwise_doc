Wwise Motion Device Initialization.

These messages provide information on how a Wwise Motion Audio Device is being initialized, and what it is being initialized as. The messages provide diagnostics on possible errors, and are not of concern if Wwise Motion is operating as expected.

If additional support is required to interpret the nature of an error, contact Audiokinetic support with information on your issue, including the associated Wwise Profiler Capture Log data.
