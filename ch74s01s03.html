Log dialog

The Log dialog displays the multi-channel files that have been generated. It also displays a generation log that contains information about any issues that occurred during the generation process. You can copy and paste the information in the log into another application for further investigation. If any issues arise during the generation of your multi-channel files, you can stop the generation process at any point.
