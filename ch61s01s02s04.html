Common tabs and categories: Busses hierarchy objects

Properties in Wwise are grouped into logical tabs in the Primary Editor and logical categories in the Property Editor. Both editors are found in Object Tabs. Across all Busses hierarchy objects, the following tabs and categories have common properties:

  • Audio Bus tab and Auxiliary Bus tab: busses to see a meter and bus status.

  • Effects tab: busses to apply one or more Effects to all objects passing through the bus.

  • States tab: busses to define custom State values for particular objects.

Additionally, the following are shared by some, but not all, of the Busses hierarchy objects:

  • Routing category: Audio and Auxiliary Busses to define properties related to the output bus, as well as game-defined and user-defined auxiliary sends, and early reflections.

  • Positioning category: Audio and Auxiliary Busses to position busses with panning or 3D positioning.

  • Advanced category: Audio Busses to define the playback limit of the voices that can pass through the bus simultaneously.

Also see Common tabs and categories: audio structures for other tabs and properties common across multiple Busses hierarchy objects.
