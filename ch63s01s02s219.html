Wwise audio out of range ...

This error occurs only if AkInitSettings::bDebugOutOfRangeCheckEnabled is enabled. It indicates an internal process is generating samples that are out of range. This always indicates an internal bug, therefore provide the full message to Audiokinetic Support.
