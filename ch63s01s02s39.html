Cannot schedule music segments: Stopping music.

There are too many segments playing or an infinite loop inside the music playlist.

Probable causes:

  • The entry and exit cue of a segment overlap.

  • Many very short segments are queued.

Recommended resolution steps:

  • Check your entry and exit cue so they don't overlap.

  • Reduce the complexity of your music container.
