Plug-in initialization failure.

This error means the listed plug-in (source, effect, mixer or sink) failed to initialize for an internal reason specific to the plug-in. This usually happens with third-party plug-in.

Probable causes:

  • Unknown, specific to the plug-in vendor.

Recommended resolution steps:

  • Look for other error messages in the Capture Log.

  • Look for messages in the Debug log of the debugger.

  • Read the documentation for this plug-in for information about specific constraints.

  • Contact the plug-in vendor.
