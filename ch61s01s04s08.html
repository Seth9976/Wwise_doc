Music Editor

The Music Editor is where you work with the contents of music objects to create varied and adaptable interactive music for your Wwise project. Here, you can arrange and edit the contents of Music Segments, assemble the contents of Music Playlist Containers, and assign contents to the Switches or States of Music Switch Containers.

The following music objects can be displayed in the Music Editor:

  • Music Switch Container Association Editor

  • Music Playlist Editor

  • Music Segment Editor
