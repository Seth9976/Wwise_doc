Setting the dynamics of HDR

Each HDR bus maintains an HDR window whose position and width is defined by the level of the loudest sound at any moment, and the project's volume threshold respectively. The behavior of the HDR window can be edited in the HDR category of each HDR bus. Since the HDR bus acts like an audio limiter/compressor, its controls are similar.
