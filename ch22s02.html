Defining the playback behavior for Random/Sequence Containers

Random and Sequence Containers give you varied options for organizing playback in your game. Random Containers allow you to create interesting soundscapes and enhance the players experience by randomly playing from a selection of objects, while Sequence Containers allow you to consistently play back objects in a designated order.
