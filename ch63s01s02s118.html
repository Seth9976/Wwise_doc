Microsoft Spatial Sound: Too many dynamic objects. Some sounds may not play.

The system doesn't have enough Audio Objects available. Any sounds intended as 3D Audio Objects that exceed the limit will be mixed in the audio bed.

Recommended resolution steps:

  • Close other applications using Audio Objects.

  • Disable the use of Audio Objects in the Wwise Authoring Audio Preferences. See Reserving system audio objects.
