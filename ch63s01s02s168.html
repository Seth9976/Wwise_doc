Seek failed due to seeking past end of file.

Pertains to the Soundseed Grain plug-in. An error occurred during plug-in execution. One of the Grains attempted to seek past the end of the file. The Grain is destroyed.

Recommended resolution steps:

  • Contact Audiokinetic Support if the error persists.
