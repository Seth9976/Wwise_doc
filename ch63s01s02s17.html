AK::SpatialAudio::SetGeometryInstance - Transform is not valid.

The transformation provided to AK::SpatialAudio::SetGeometryInstance is not defined properly.

Probable cause:

  • This issue is caused by either the input position not being a valid vector or the orientation vectors not being normalized and/or orthogonal.
