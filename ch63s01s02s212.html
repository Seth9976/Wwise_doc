Unsupported encoding, only PCM, ADPCM, and Vorbis are supported.

Pertains to the Soundseed Grain plug-in. The media file's encoding is not supported by the plug-in.

Recommended resolution steps:

  • Change the media file's Conversion Settings in the Wwise project.

  • Regenerate the SoundBanks.
