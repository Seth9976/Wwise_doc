ADPCM Parameters

In the ADCPM Parameters dialog you can set the Loop fix parameter of the ADPCM codec. Platinum Games Inc. courteously provided Audiokinetic the ADPCM encoder and decoder used in Wwise.

Interface Element

Description

ADPCM Encoding Parameters

Loop fix

The list of methods for aligning loop markers that are not on sample boundaries.

  • Pitch Shifting: To accelerate or slow down the pitch to fit the sample boundary for the conversion of the file, based on the platform.

  • Interpolation/Elimination: To add or remove samples to fit the sample boundary for the conversion of the file, based on the platform.

Closes the ADPCM Parameters dialog and saves your settings.

Closes the ADPCM Parameters dialog without saving your settings.
